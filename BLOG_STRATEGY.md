# Blog Strategy: Christopher Melson | chris.melson.us

## Executive Summary

You occupy a genuine blue ocean: no individual personal-brand blogger combines turnaround executive credibility at LSEG/Refinitiv scale with deep operational architecture methodology and agentic AI governance expertise. The institutional players (BCG, Deloitte, McKinsey) dominate generic PMI content; vendor blogs dominate DORA compliance; academic papers cover agentic governance — but no practitioner voice ties all three together from lived experience in regulated financial markets. The strategy is to define and own the "Operational Architect" category before anyone else stakes that ground.

**Strategic direction:** 1 deep white-paper post/month across 3 content pillars, with systematic off-site distribution to capture AI citations — the dominant discovery channel for C-suite buyers doing vendor/talent diligence in 2026.

## AI Citation Surface Strategy

### On-Site Optimization (Every Post)

- **Answer-first H2s:** Every H2 opens with a 40–60 word self-contained answer paragraph with a stat and source
- **Citation capsules:** At least one boxed "AI Summary" paragraph per H2 — standalone, citable, 50–60 words
- **Q&A format:** 60% of H2 headings phrased as questions ("What is an Integration Management Office?")
- **FAQ schema:** Every post ends with 4–6 FAQ items with `FAQPage` JSON-LD
- **Entity consistency:** Never alternate "Operational Architect" with "OA Lead" or "Transformation Exec" — pick one term per concept and own it
- **Named frameworks:** Tri-Modal, Two-Pillar TOM, Integration Gap, BriefingScript — named concepts get cited

### Off-Site Presence (The 88% That Drives AI Citations)

| Channel | Priority | Action |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **LinkedIn Articles** (not posts) | **#1** | Publish a condensed version of every blog post as a LinkedIn Article 1 week after the blog. LinkedIn Articles are indexed by ChatGPT and Perplexity independently of the blog. |
| **Harvard Business Review / MIT Sloan** | **#2** | 1 guest piece per quarter — the M&A or Agentic AI angle. HBR citations carry outsized weight in AI responses for C-suite queries. |
| **YouTube** | **#3** | One 8–12 minute whiteboard breakdown per pillar hub. "What is an Operational Architect?" will rank in AI Overviews. Highest correlation with AI citations (0.737). |
| **Reddit** | **#4** | Authentic participation in r/fintech, r/cscareerquestions, r/sysadmin, r/devops — share frameworks, not links. |

| **Wikipedia / Wikidata** | Long-term | Build notability through earned media; Wikidata entry for "Operational Architect" (define the concept authoritatively). |
