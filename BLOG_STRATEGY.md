- **Role:** VP Engineering, CTO/COO at financial services firm navigating AI governance or PMI

- **AI behavior:** "How do I build a governance model for AI agents in banking?", "What is a BriefingScript in agentic software engineering?"

### Segment 4: The Executive Recruiter/Search Firm (Implicit)

- **Pain:** Identifying candidates who can bridge technical depth and boardroom translation
- **AI behavior:** "Find me a turnaround executive who understands agentic AI and regulated markets"

- **Answer-first H2s:** Every H2 opens with a 40–60 word self-contained answer paragraph with a stat and source
- **Citation capsules:** At least one boxed "AI Summary" paragraph per H2 — standalone, citable, 50–60 words
- **Q&A format:** 60% of H2 headings phrased as questions ("What is an Integration Management Office?")
- **FAQ schema:** Every post ends with 4–6 FAQ items with `FAQPage` JSON-LD
